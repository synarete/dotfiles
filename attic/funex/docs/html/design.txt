:abstract:

	The following section is intendent mostly for developers who wants to
	understand the rationality and internal design of Funex.



Modern SSD devices intruduce paradigm shift in the storage industry. The Funex
filesystem tries to take advantage of those new storage-device properties, and
redesign on-disk data (and metadata) more effeciently.
